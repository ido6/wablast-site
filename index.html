FAQ

Good questions

Can my number still get banned?

Yes — driving WhatsApp Web is against WhatsApp's Terms, and any number can be banned. WaBlast reduces risk with conservative pacing, warm-up, and caps, but it is not a guarantee. Use a number you can afford to lose, warm it up, and message only people who opted in.

How does the pacing work?

Messages go out one at a time with a randomized 8–30s gap, a longer pause every batch, a per-day cap (with a warm-up ramp for new numbers), and only inside your working hours. Sending auto-pauses if the session drops or errors spike.

Do you handle opt-outs?

Automatically. When someone replies a stop keyword (STOP, UNSUBSCRIBE, הסר…), that contact is flagged and skipped in every future campaign.

What do I need to start?

A WhatsApp number and your contact list as Excel or CSV. You scan a QR once to connect, then import and send.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Plans are month-to-month (or annual if you choose). Cancel whenever — no lock-in.
